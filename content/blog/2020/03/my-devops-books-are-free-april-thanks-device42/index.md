I especially thank Device42 for their gesture today, which sustains my open source development and projects like my new [Ansible 101 live stream series](/blog/2020/ansible-101-jeff-geerling-youtube-streaming-series)! Their action helps me provide for my family while working on my open source projects and books (even while giving them away), and helps thousands of others who may benefit from the books, software, and media I help produce.

Thank you _so_ much to Device42 for sponsoring another month of giving away the books for free!

As a reminder: with LeanPub, _every_ update to the book I make, forever, is free. Ansible for Kubernetes is still being written, so you don't get a 100% complete book _today_, but you can download the updated book as it's written, and as with Ansible for DevOps, I intend to keep it updated as long as I'm able, even after it's complete!
