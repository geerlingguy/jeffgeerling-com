> **Update**: The upgrade was a success! The site is now on Drupal 9.0.0, yay!

I'm planning on upgrading my [www.pidramble.com](https://www.pidramble.com) website from Drupal 8 to Drupal 9 LIVE on my YouTube channel today, in honor of the [#D9Launch](https://twitter.com/hashtag/d9launch) today!

The site is currently running on a single Raspberry Pi on my desk in my basement... so this would not only be the world's first _live_ Drupal 9 upgrade, it may possibly be the first-ever Drupal 9 site running on a Raspberry Pi!
