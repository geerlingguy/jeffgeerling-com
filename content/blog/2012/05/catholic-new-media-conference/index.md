I'll be attending the <a href="http://cnmc.sqpn.com/">2012 Catholic New Media Conference</a> in Dallas/Fort Worth from August 29-31; will I see you there? This year will be the largest CNMC yet, with a ton of great speakers and separate tracks for different days of the conference.

<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://cnmc.sqpn.com/">{{< figure src="./cnmc-2012.png" alt="CNMC 2012" width="460" height="71" class="blog-image" >}}</a></p>

This year's <a href="http://cnmc.sqpn.com/marketing-your-message/">theme</a> is <em>Marketing Your Message</em>, and the main track of the CNMC Professional Day will be filled with keynote speakers talking about this theme. At the same time, <a href="http://fallibleblogma.com/">Matthew Warner</a> and I will be leading a <a href="http://cnmc.sqpn.com/2012/05/21/cnmc-tech-summit-announced/">Tech Summit</a>, meant for Catholic app and web developers, designers, and Catholic development companies, talking about apps, APIs, web development, and collaboration.

I'll probably talk a bit about the <a href="https://github.com/geerlingguy/Catholic-Diocese-App">Catholic Diocese App</a> (with some great new news on that front to be announced!), <a href="http://www.opensourcecatholic.com/">Open Source Catholic</a>, and some other projects and topics near and dear to my heart.
