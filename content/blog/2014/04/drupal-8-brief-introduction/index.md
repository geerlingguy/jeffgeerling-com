<p>I presented <a href="http://www.slideshare.net/geerlingguy/drupal-8-a-brief-introduction">Drupal 8 - A Brief Introduction</a> at <a href="http://2014.drupalstl.org/">DrupalCamp STL.14</a> on April 26, 2014.</p>

<p>Drupal 8 brings a lot of changes. Many standby contributed modules are now included with Drupal Core, and many small changes add up to the most exciting Drupal release yet! This presentation guides you through many of the biggest changes, highlighting how Drupal 8 will accelerate your web development and provide tools to make Drupal the best content management platform on any device.</p>
