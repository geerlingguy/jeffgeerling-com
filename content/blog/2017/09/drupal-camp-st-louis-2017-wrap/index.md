The St. Louis Drupal Users Group ([STLDUG](https://www.drupalstl.org)) just finished it's [fourth Drupal Camp](https://2017.drupalstl.org), held at UMSL yesterday. I had a great time meeting with everyone, and am excited for next year! Last year I had to miss the Camp due to unexpected surgery, but this year I was able to attend and even bring some of my photo gear, to take pictures (I love contributing to open source through means other than code!); here's the obligatory 'whole camp' photo:
