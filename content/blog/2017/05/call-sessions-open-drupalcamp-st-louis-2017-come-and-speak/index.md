[DrupalCamp St. Louis 2017](https://2017.drupalstl.org) will be held **September 22-23, 2017**, in St. Louis, Missouri. This will be our fourth year hosting a DrupalCamp, and we're one of the best camps for new presenters!

If you did something amazing with Drupal, if you're an aspiring themer, site builder, or developer, or if you are working on making the web a better place, we'd love for you to [**submit a session**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSepKI6mquZzUMh3rfDHxbkeCM70okPzjNOS9Tf9TRv7-tufmA/viewform?usp=sf_link). Session submissions are due by August 1.
